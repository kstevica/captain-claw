The recognition of Nuqleus and ZICER as top European startup hubs by the Financial Times validates Croatia's position as a serious player in the continental startup ecosystem.

Policy and regulation also feature prominently, with Croatia's delayed implementation of the EU AI Act raising concerns about developer liability and competitiveness. Simultaneously, the .hr domain's strong security reputation and recent growth (18,000 new registrations) reflect the region's commitment to digital trust and infrastructure quality. Overall, the front page presents a snapshot of an emerging tech powerhouse navigating the complexities of rapid digital transformation, global competition, and the need for inclusive, equitable growth.
